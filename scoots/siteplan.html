Site Name

Northwest Oregon Chamber of Commerce

I chose this name because it's a Chamber of Commerce, for my general area. There's already a Chamber of Commerce for my immediate area, so I chose a broader name.

Site Purpose

The Northwest Oregon Chamber of Commerce is for learning and developing business skills and facilitating collaboration and shared growth between local businesses.

Scenarios

  • What kinds of businesses are there in the area?
  • How can I develop my own business?
  • What are some local events going on?
